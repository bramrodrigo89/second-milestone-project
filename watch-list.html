Instructions

My Watch List is a simulation tool that allows you to create a fictional portfolio with the stock shares and ETFs of your choice. No need to create an account with us!

The information you enter on your Watch List is saved solely on your browser: it is not shared with anyone else. Changes can be done on My Watch List any time!

Simply click on the star symbol to remove or add again a stock from your list. Use the symbols to add or to remove one or more shares to your portfolio.
